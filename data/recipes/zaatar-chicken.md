## Ingredients

- Boneless skinless chicken breasts (2, butterflied and cut into strips)
- Oil (2 Tbsp + 2 tsp, divided)
- Lemon (1, juiced)
- Za'atar (1/4 cup)
- Garlic cloves (8, minced)
- Kosher salt (2 tsp)
- Toasted sesame oil (1 tsp)
- Freshly ground black pepper (1 tsp)

## Instructions

- Whisk 2 Tbsp oil, lemon juice, za'atar, garlic, salt, sesame oil, and pepper together in a bowl.

- Add chicken to bowl and toss to evenly coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.

- Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) fan.

- Heat 2 tsp oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated chicken until golden brown, about 4-5 minutes per side.

- Transfer skillet to the oven and roast until internal temperature reaches 165°F, approximately 15-20 minutes.

- Remove from oven and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
